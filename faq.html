Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Online Voting System?
The Online Voting System allows registered users to vote in elections through a secure web platform from any device with an internet connection.
How can I register to vote?
You can register to vote by visiting the registration page, entering your details, and verifying your email address.
Is my vote confidential?
Yes, all votes cast through the Online Voting System are anonymous and encrypted to ensure confidentiality.
Can I vote multiple times?
No, each user can vote only once per election. The system ensures that duplicate votes are prevented.
What if I forget my password?
If you forget your password, you can reset it using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
